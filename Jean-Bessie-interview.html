Jean-Baptiste Besancon

Could you tell us a little about yourself? How did your work in art begin?


I'm a London based painter specialising in abstract and street photography.
I'm best known for using a bold palette to help illustrate the vibrancy of life, whilst also exploring themes of diversity and disparity. My work often enters into abstraction and presents the viewer with an unfamiliar view of a familiar city.
I host regular painting workshops in London and sell limited art prints to fine art collectors, enthusiasts, companies and interior designers around the world.
